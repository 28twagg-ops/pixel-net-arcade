HOW TO PLAY

Move: WASD keys

Aim/Shoot: Arrow keys (hold to auto‑fire; diagonal shots allowed)

Goal: Survive waves of enemies in a neon arena. Chain kills quickly to increase your combo multiplier and rack up points faster.

Tip: Keep moving to dodge incoming foes and bullets. Chasing combos is risky – getting greedy can end your run!

Initials are set on the homepage. Click the badge (top-right) to change.
