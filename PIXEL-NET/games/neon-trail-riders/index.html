HOW TO PLAY

Move: Arrow Keys / WASD

Goal: Navigate the glowing grid without crashing into your own trail or the arena walls. Collect orbs to clear part of your trail and earn bonus points.

Tip: Plan ahead and use orbs to carve out breathing room when the arena gets tight.

Initials are set on the homepage. Click the badge (top-right) to change.
